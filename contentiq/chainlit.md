# Welcome to Contentiq — Your AI Writing Partner

**Contentiq** is a professional AI content writing assistant powered by GPT-4o. It produces clean, structured, and audience-targeted content on demand.

---

## What Contentiq Writes

| Content Type | Examples |
|---|---|
| Blog & Articles | Long-form posts, SEO articles, how-to guides |
| Social Media | Instagram captions, LinkedIn posts, Twitter/X threads |
| Marketing Copy | Landing page copy, ad copy, taglines, CTAs |
| Email Campaigns | Newsletters, cold outreach, follow-up sequences |
| Product Content | Descriptions, feature highlights, Amazon listings |
| Business Writing | Proposals, bios, company descriptions, pitches |
| Fiverr Gigs | Titles, descriptions, package copy, FAQs |

---

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---|---|
| `/tone [name]` | Switch writing tone: `Professional` · `Casual` · `Persuasive` · `Friendly` |
| `/format [name]` | Switch output format: `Paragraph` · `Bullet Points` · `Numbered List` |
| `/reset` | Clear conversation history and start fresh |

---

## Tips for Better Results

- **Specify your audience** — "Write for freelance designers aged 25–35"
- **Define the purpose** — "This is for a LinkedIn post to attract B2B clients"
- **Use action buttons** — Click **Regenerate** or **Make it shorter** after any response
- **Chain commands** — Switch tone to Persuasive before writing ad copy for better results
